About Me

My name is David Yan and I am currently a 2nd year student at Duke University. I hope to get a BS degree in Computer Science with a minor in Statistical Science.

I am currently taking classes pertaining to Computer Architecture and Web Design Principles, but I hope to take more Computer science classes that have strong mathematical and statistical applications. More information about the Computer Science courses I take and those offered at Duke can be viewed here at the Duke Computer Science Department's webpage.

I am most interested in machine learning and modern applications of Artificial Intelligence, and hope to work in these fields in the future. In particular, projects like Deep Blue, the first chess playing computer to beat a human world champion chess player, are my favorite applications of AI.
